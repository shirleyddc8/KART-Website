ABOUT US

Kart Films began its journey in the 1990s as part of Kaleidoscope, a creative collective known for transforming ideas into vibrant visual experiences.

Today, Kart enters a new chapter under the leadership of Leong, carrying forward decades of production expertise while embracing a sharper focus on storytelling, collaboration and execution.
